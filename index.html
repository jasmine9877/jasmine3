About Me

I am a person who likes to observe the details of life, and I am used to using images and designs to record those small but moving moments in daily life. For me, creation is not just an expression, but a way to talk to the world. Whether it's photography, graphic design or handmade art, I'm always willing to experiment and find balance and fun in my work.
